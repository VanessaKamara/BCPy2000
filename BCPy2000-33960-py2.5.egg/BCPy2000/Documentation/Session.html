Session

Definition: session

Literally meaning "sitting", we use this term to mean a period lasting anything from 1 to 5 hours in which, typically, a subject turns up, performs several runs, gets paid and goes away again. For EEG, a "session" should also be considered to have ended when the cap is taken off, since re-application of the cap even on the same afternoon will probably lead to a very different data distribution from before (one example of a "session-to-session transfer" problem).

BCI2000 data file names usually end in SsssRrr.dat where sss denotes the session number and rr denotes the run number within the session.
