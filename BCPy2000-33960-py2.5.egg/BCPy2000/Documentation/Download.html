Download from the Website

The way to get the latest version of BCPy2000 is to check it out from the bci2000.org subversion repository as part of the complete BCI2000 source distribution.

Alternatively, for pre-built binaries and installers (which may be slightly less up-to-date than the svn version) and for installers of BCPy2000's third-party dependencies, go to http://bci2000.org/downloads/BCPy2000/Download.html to see the latest information.
