Video timing

The same measurement experiment we use for assessing event timing also alternates the colour of a small patch between black and white on every frame, and measures the frame timing in milliseconds. Dropped frames are visible as a flash, and usually stand out in the timing logs.

So far we have been unable to eliminate entirely the phenomenon of occasional dropped frames. These may be a function of the fact that we are running VisionEgg in one of a number of parallel threads (Python is notorious for its "global interpreter lock" which makes true multi-threading impossible). So we might have to wait for all our dependencies to catch up to Python 2.6+, where multi-process support is available as an alternative to multi-threading, before this can be solved completely.
